As a self-taught web developer, I never went through any formal schooling for programming or development. While being self-taught takes tenacity and hard work, it left me with some gaps in knowledge. That was when I decided to enroll in this web development bootcamp.

Before attending the bootcamp, I had experience with HTML, CSS, Python (particularly the Django framework) and some JavaScript. Throughout the bootcamp I picked up a lot of more JavaScript, in addition to Node.JS.

As someone who comes from a content background and someone who lived a life as a past journalist, web development offers an interesting pivot as I have seen the tech industry from the shoes of other roles. I landed on pivoting my career to being a fulltime dev.
